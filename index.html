Vimerk is a solo digital products company based in Uppsala, Sweden. We build small, precise, and durable technical products: APIs, applied machine-learning tools, and AI-assisted services that solve real problems with minimal operational overhead.

This is not a startup chasing scale for its own sake. It's a long-term, independent engineering practice—productized.

Most digital products fail not because the ideas are bad, but because they are overengineered, overpromised, and overdependent on complexity or constant intervention. Vimerk exists to do the opposite.

Principles

Solo-Engineered, Solo-Maintained

If one person can't build, deploy, and maintain it with minimal ongoing attention, it doesn't belong at Vimerk.

Narrow Scope, Clear Boundaries

Each product solves one well-defined problem. Ambiguity is a liability, not a feature.

AI as Tool, Not Identity

Used where it adds genuine leverage. Avoided where deterministic or statistical methods are superior. Never an excuse for opacity.

Built to Last, Not to Exit

Every project must stand alone economically—able to generate revenue independently or be shut down without threatening the rest.

What We Don't Build

  • No venture-capital dependency
  • No growth-at-all-costs strategies
  • No regulatory grey zones
